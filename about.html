About kaspa.best

A free, independent place to actually understand Kaspa — what a BlockDAG is, how GHOSTDAG orders blocks, how mining works, and how to hold KAS safely. No hype, no token sales, nothing to buy from us.

What this is

An explainer, not a promoter

Most crypto sites either assume you already know everything, or they're trying to sell you something. This one tries to do the boring, useful thing: explain the technology clearly, show it moving, and let you poke at it yourself.

Interactive, not static

The BlockDAG visualizer, the GHOSTDAG walkthrough and the emission curve are all live and interactive. Reading about parallel blocks is one thing — watching them merge is another.

Honest about risk

Nothing here is financial advice. Mining maths, tokenomics and price data are shown so you can reason for yourself, not to convince you to buy anything.

Runs in your browser

The tools are client-side. The watch-only wallet reads public addresses from a public API — it never asks for, stores or transmits a private key or seed phrase.

What's here

The tools

BlockDAG visualizer

See parallel blocks, merge sets and the selected parent chain form in real time — the clearest way to grasp what makes Kaspa different from a single-chain blockchain.

Open the visualizer →

Watch-only wallet

Paste any kaspa: address to track its balance and transactions. Read-only by design: no keys, no seed, nothing to lose.

Experimental wallet

An in-development, browser-based wallet built for learning how key derivation and addresses actually work. Clearly labelled experimental — don't put real funds in it.
