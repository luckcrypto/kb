Kaspa watch-only wallet

Paste any kaspa: address to track its live balance, USD value, UTXOs and recent transactions. It only ever reads public data — there is no seed phrase, no private key, and nothing to steal. Save your watchlist to a file and load it back anytime.

✓ No keys, ever ✓ Can't spend your coins ✓ Runs in your browser ✓ Data from api.kaspa.org

Why you can trust this tool

Read-only by design

🔑

Zero key handling

This page has no field for a seed phrase or private key, and no code path that could use one. It physically cannot move your funds. If a "watch-only" tool ever asks for your seed, close it — it's a scam.

📡

Straight from the source

Balances, UTXOs and history are fetched directly by your browser from the public Kaspa REST API at api.kaspa.org. No kaspa.best server sits in the middle, and nothing about your visit is logged.

🗂️

Your data, your device

Your watchlist lives only in this browser tab. Save it to a JSON file on your own machine and load it back whenever — the same way a portfolio file works. Nothing is uploaded.

Please read — important

  • This is a watch-only tool. It shows public information only and is not financial advice. Prices and balances may be delayed or, if the data feed is down, temporarily unavailable.
  • A watch-only wallet never needs your secrets. No legitimate wallet, exchange, "support agent", airdrop or giveaway will ever ask for your seed phrase or private key. Every such request is a scam.
  • Double-check addresses. Anyone can paste any address here to view it — seeing a balance does not prove ownership. Only trust an address you generated or were given through a channel you trust.
  • Public data is public. A Kaspa address and its full history are visible to everyone on-chain. Watching an address here doesn't reveal anything that isn't already public, but be mindful of linking addresses to your identity.
FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is a watch-only wallet safe?
Yes. It only needs a public address. It never asks for and never handles your seed phrase or private keys, so it cannot spend or move funds and there's nothing for it to leak. If any tool calls itself watch-only but asks for your seed, it's a scam.
Where does the data come from?
Balances, UTXOs and transactions are fetched directly by your browser from the public Kaspa REST API at api.kaspa.org. There's no kaspa.best server in between, and nothing about your visit is logged.
Can this wallet send Kaspa?
No. By design it has no access to keys and cannot create, sign or broadcast a transaction. It only reads public on-chain data for the addresses you paste in.
What's a UTXO?
Kaspa, like Bitcoin, is UTXO-based. Your balance is the sum of unspent transaction outputs (UTXOs) locked to your address. The card shows how many UTXOs make up the balance.
Does it remember my addresses?
Only in this tab while it's open. Nothing is uploaded. Use “Save watchlist” to keep a file on your device and “Load watchlist” to bring it back later.
